Post-CABG Risk Scoring



Pre-operative factors



1. Age

2. Hypertension

3. Anaemia (Hb less than 12.5mg/dl)

4. eGFR less than 60 ml/min



Intra-operative factors



5. RBC Transfusion

6. Use of Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump

7. Cardiopulmonary bypass more than or equal to 120 min

8. Lowest haematocrit during cardiopulmonary bypass less than 22%





Categories of AKI risk:

Low: 0-3 points

Medium: 4-8 points

High: 9-13 points
